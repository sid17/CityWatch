City Watch is a Android application that gives citizens more efficient way to notify the infrastructural issues in the locality.It enables users to systematically formulate the report and submit it to the City. The location and the citizen's description allow the system to route the issue to appropriate department for resolution,while also enabling citizens to track the progress of their request.Users can review requests of their locality to guarantee its authenticity.
Submit A Report:
1) Select type of report
2) Add an image(optional)
3) Update your location
4) Add description
5) Post your report
Recent Updates:
- Review and verify reports in your locality. Reports verified once by a different user are lodged as complaints.
- Check out the status of your update.
- Check out all updates.
-Notation:
Red => reports not yet processed
Blue => report under process
Green =>issue resolved
Statistics:
Obtain a graphical and data analysis of various reports reported over different categories over different wards.
